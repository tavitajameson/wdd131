NBA Fan Hub – Site Plan

Site Name

NBA Fan Hub

This name was chosen because it clearly communicates the website's purpose: a central hub for NBA fans to explore teams, players, and league information.

Site Purpose

The purpose of NBA Fan Hub is to provide NBA fans with an organized, visually appealing, and interactive website to learn about teams, players, and league information. It will include dynamic JavaScript-driven content, responsive design, images, and interactive features such as a form and localStorage-based functionality.

Scenarios

  • "Which NBA team should I learn more about, and what are their key details?"
  • "Who are some of the most notable players in the league, and what makes them stand out?"

Color Schema

Main Colors:

  • NBA Red (#c8102e) – used for accents, buttons, and highlights.
  • NBA Blue (#1d428a)

    • Used for headings, borders, and navigation highlights.

Typography

Fonts chosen:

  • Arial – used for body text, paragraphs, and navigation.
  • Verdana – used for headings and accents.
